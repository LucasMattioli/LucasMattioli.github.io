Hobbies

I've been dancing Lindy Hop since 2019 and fell in love with it right away. Since then, I've been taking classes and traveling to various international festivals. From the start I explored both roles—lead & follow—and I also love Solo Jazz (aka Jazz Roots). In 2023 I joined a crew: the Swingyditeam (then called Swingydibobidi-boo). Together we created routines for the Savoy Cup and ILHC Europe. Here are a few of our videos.
