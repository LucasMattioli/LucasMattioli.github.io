About

I am a PhD candidate co‑supervised by Stéphane Herbin (ONERA) and Faouzi Adjed (IRT SystemX). My research focuses on making multimodal foundation models (especially Vision–Language Models) reliable under tight data constraints through PEFT (LoRA/adapters), prompt learning, in‑context learning, and distillation.
